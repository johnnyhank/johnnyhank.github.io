Beyond research, I enjoy sports, music, reading, and a few quiet forms of craft.

## Sports

**Working out**: Regular gym training for fitness and stress relief.

**Running**: A quiet way to clear my mind and build endurance.

**Swimming**: Relaxing, rhythmic, and physically demanding.

## Music

Music has been part of my life since childhood, both as a performer and as a listener.

**Traditional Chinese Music**: I have played the bamboo flute since the age of eight, earned a Level 10 certificate, and am a member of the Shanghai Jiao Tong University Student Traditional Orchestra.

**Musical Taste**: My taste leans toward expressive, free-spirited music with lyrical depth, including artists such as Faye Wong, Pu Shu, Li Jian, and Prince.

## Reading

I like literature that combines philosophical reflection with social insight.

**Favorite Works**:
- *我的地坛* — Life, disability, and meaning
- *ノルウェイの森* — Youth, memory, love, and loss
- *La Porte étroite* — Love, sacrifice, and spiritual pursuit
